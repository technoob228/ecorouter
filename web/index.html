FAQ

Is this just OpenRouter with a fee on top?
Yes, essentially. We route through OpenRouter and add a transparent 9% fee, of which 5% is provably donated to eco funds on-chain. We don't pretend to be something we're not.
Why should I pay 9% more?
You shouldn't if you don't care about offsetting AI's environmental impact. We're for developers who do. 9% on gpt-4o-mini is ~$0.04 per million tokens — less than a rounding error in most budgets.
How do I know the money actually goes to charities?
Every eco wallet transaction is on the Tron blockchain. You can see the wallet address, every incoming fee, and every outgoing donation on Tronscan. We can't fake it even if we wanted to.
What if I send the wrong amount?
Use the /v1/auth/claim-deposit endpoint with your tx_hash. We'll match it manually.
Can I choose which fund receives my contribution?
Not individually. We rotate monthly between three funds tied to AI's specific environmental damage: COTAP (carbon from electricity), WaterAid (water from cooling), Oceanic Society (ecosystems). Full cycle every 3 months.
Who runs this?
One developer. Open source project. No VC funding, no corporation. Just an API proxy, two public wallets, and a commitment to transparency.
What happens if the project shuts down?
Remaining eco wallet balance gets donated in full. The wallet is public — you can verify this happened. Code is open source — anyone can fork and continue.
